[ydl-sideload]
[tutorial]
====================================================
How to sideload a .msixbundle app with ydl-sideload:
1. Download your .msixbundle app from a trusted
source (ie. GitHub)
2. Paste the file here (in this folder) and rename
it to "ydl-sideload.msixbundle".
3. Run the script with "run.cmd" in the main folder.
